# Executive Summary

## Overview
The Credit Underwriting Intelligence Suite (CUIS) is an enterprise decision-support platform designed to automate the commercial lending lifecycle. It bridges the gap between unstructured financial data (PDFs) and actionable risk intelligence.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Automated Data Extraction:** Eliminates manual data entry from Audited Financials, GST Returns, and Bank Statements.
- **Rule-Based Decisioning:** Replaces subjective analysis with a deterministic Credit Policy Engine.
- **Standardized Outputs:** Generates bank-compliant Excel Credit Assessment Memos (CAMs) and interactive HTML dashboards in under 2 minutes.

## Target Audience
- Credit Risk Officers
- Business / Financial Analysts
- Commercial Loan Underwriters

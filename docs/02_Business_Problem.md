# The Business Problem

## The Status Quo
In traditional commercial lending, underwriter analysts face significant bottlenecks:
1. **Data Fragmentation:** Financial health signals are scattered across Income Tax Returns (ITR), Goods & Services Tax (GST) filings, and massive multi-month bank ledgers.
2. **Manual Effort:** Analysts spend 2 to 3 days manually copy-pasting numbers from scanned PDFs into legacy Excel spread-sheets.
3. **Calculation Errors:** Complex reconciliations (like adjusting Creditor Days based on actual manufacturing expenses rather than raw purchases) are prone to human error.
4. **Hidden Risks:** Detecting diversion of funds across 10,000+ bank transactions using visual sampling is nearly impossible.

## The CUIS Solution
CUIS transforms this fragmented, manual workflow into a centralized, automated pipeline. By treating underwriting as a deterministic data problem rather than a subjective human task, CUIS ensures 100% policy compliance, auditability, and speed.

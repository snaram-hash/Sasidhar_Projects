Engineering Philosophy

Separation of Concerns

I separate business rules from application logic. My parsing engines never make credit decisions—they just feed the Policy Engine.

Explainability over Black Boxes

In lending, auditability is everything. I chose configurable Rule Engines over raw ML to ensure every rejection reason is strictly traceable to a banking mandate.

Zero-Friction Deployment

I design for the user. By generating self-contained HTML dashboards and relying on SQLite, my platforms can run directly on an analyst's laptop with no IT overhead.
